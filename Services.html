Services

Masikap Refrigeration and Air-conditioning Services (MRAS) is offering the following services:

  • Repair and troubleshooting of refrigeration and air-conditioning i.e., Window Type, Split Type, Package Type and Freezers.
  • Installation of New Air-conditioning units such as window type, split type, and package air-conditioning unit.
  • Repair and rehabilitation of air-handling units, cooling tower and air duct system.
  • Reconditioning of drive motor and pumps.
  • Over hauling of reciprocating compressor and centrifugal compressor.
  • Installation of exhaust fan and air ventilation.
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance of split type and package type air-conditioning.
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance of package chiller such as reciprocating compressor, screw type compressor.
  • Preventive Maintenance of centralized air-conditioning equipment's such as centrifugal chiller, chilled water pump, condenser water pump, air handling unit, cooling tower, fan coil units.

Central Air Conditioner

Central air conditioner serves as the most effective unit when choosing an air conditioner. It is the main air cooling system of a room or workplace which functions to circulate cool air through the supply ducts and registers and also dehumidify the air to improve comfort and better ambiance.

Ductless Mini-Split Air Conditioner

Mini split-system air conditioners (mini-splits) are ductless air conditioners that can be used in residential, commercial, and institutional buildings. Multifamily housing and retrofit add-ons to houses with "non-ducted" heating systems, such as hydronic (hot water heat), radiant panels, and space heaters, are the most typical applications (wood, kerosene, propane).

Window Air Conditioner

The term "room air conditioner" is also used to describe a window air conditioner. It's the most basic type of air conditioner, and it's fixed on windows or walls. It is a single unit that is assembled in a case that contains all of the components.

Portable Air Conditioner

Portable air conditioner, as its name suggests, has only one distinct characteristic than the other units which is its portability. Portable units are also famous for those people who live in smaller spaces and cannot afford to buy ductwork for central air units or whose apartment is not allowed to use window-mounted units.

Floor Mounted Air Conditioner

Another form of air conditioner device that is often used in restaurants, halls, motels, and data centers is the floor air conditioner. Its name comes from the fact that it is essentially standing on the floor. Indoor units can also be wall-mounted, ducted, ceiling-exposed, portable, or window-mounted.

Hybrid Fuel Air Conditioner

A dual fuel air conditioner heats and cools the room while also providing a backup heating system that runs on a different fuel source. As a result, this system can be used to cool and heat your home. One single system meets all of your requirements, making system maintenance easier. It transfers heat from one location to another, keeping the temperature cool.

Smart Air Conditioner

Smart air conditioners, unlike traditional air conditioners, allow you to control the temperature of your home using your smartphone. You can control their operation with an app that you may download to your phone or tablet. Additionally, they can be linked to smart home systems and voice assistants.
