Our Story

Masikap Refrigeration and Air-conditioning Services (MRAS) is a Refrigeration Air-conditioning Company organized on 15th of January 2014, dedicated on providing the highest quality service and installations at the most competitive cost.

We specialize in residential, commercial and industrial air conditioning.
